Drop your photos in this folder.

The gallery in src/data/config.ts points to files like:
  /gallery/01.jpg, /gallery/02.jpg, ...

Name your files to match (01.jpg, 02.jpg, ...) or edit the `src`
values in config.ts to match your filenames.

Until you add real photos, elegant gradient placeholders are shown
automatically — nothing will ever look broken.

Tips:
  • Portrait images (3:4) look best in the polaroid cards.
  • Keep each file under ~500 KB for fast mobile loading.
